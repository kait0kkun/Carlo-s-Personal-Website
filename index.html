About Me

Bridging Publishing & Education

A seasoned sales leader passionate about making quality educational materials accessible across the Philippines.

Empowering Education Through Strategic Publishing

With over 15 years of experience in the Philippine publishing industry, I have dedicated my career to ensuring that quality educational materials reach every corner of the nation. As the Vice President of Sales at Lampara Publishing House, Inc., I lead strategic initiatives that bridge the gap between publishers and educational institutions.

My work spans collaborating with the Department of Education (DepEd), managing nationwide distribution networks, and building lasting partnerships with schools, regional offices, and government agencies. I believe that every Filipino learner deserves access to reliable, engaging, and standards-aligned learning resources.

Beyond sales, I am passionate about literacy advocacy, curriculum development support, and fostering a culture of reading across communities. Featured at the 2025 Regional Brigada Eskwela Kick-Off, I continue to champion the role of publishers in building a stronger educational foundation for the Philippines.
